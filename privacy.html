Privacy Policy - Space Invaders II

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Space Invaders II ("the App") is a mobile arcade game developed by Eduardo Figueiredo.

Information We Collect

The App collects only the player name and game score voluntarily entered by the user for the global leaderboard feature. No personal identification information is collected.

How We Use Information

Player names and scores are stored in a secure database solely for the purpose of displaying the global leaderboard within the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Supabase for leaderboard data storage. Please refer to Supabase's privacy policy at https://supabase.com/privacy for more information.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond the voluntary game score submission described above.
